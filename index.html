Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

A complete AI testing toolkit that fits into your workflow — no infrastructure, no cloud account, no lock-in.

✨

AI Test Generation

Paste a URL or describe what to test in plain English. Mendex inspects the page and writes the full test plan for you — including assertions, form fills, and navigation.

⏺

Click-to-Record

Open a browser, click through your workflow, and Mendex captures every interaction. The recording is instantly converted into a replayable, editable YAML test plan.

🔄

Self-Healing Tests

When a selector breaks after a UI change, Mendex automatically tries ARIA roles, visible text, and LLM-generated CSS — often without you even noticing the fix.

🔐

Smart Login Automation

Configure credentials once. Mendex detects the login form — even behind a "Sign In" button click or inside an iframe — fills the fields, and proceeds to your test.

🎬

Video Recording

Every test run is recorded as a video. Watch exactly what happened on any step — invaluable for debugging failures without having to reproduce them manually.

⏱

Scheduled Runs

Set up a schedule and let Mendex run your test suite every 15 minutes, hourly, or daily. Results and history are saved locally so you always know when something broke.

📄

Human-readable test plans

Tests are stored as plain YAML files you can read, edit, and version-control. No proprietary format, no database — just files in your project folder.

You can also edit steps directly inside the app with the built-in step editor — no YAML knowledge required.

# checkout-flow.mendex.yml name: Checkout flow steps: - action: navigate target: https://shop.example.com/cart - action: click target: button[data-testid="checkout"] - action: fill target: input[name="email"] value: test@example.com - action: assert type: text target: .order-summary expected: Order confirmed

Self-Healing AI

Tests that fix
themselves

UI changes constantly. CSS classes get renamed, IDs change, components restructure. Mendex handles it automatically so your tests stay green even as your codebase evolves.

When a step fails to find an element, Mendex tries three increasingly powerful recovery strategies before marking the test as failed.

♿
ARIA Role Matching
Finds elements by their semantic role and accessible label — resilient to class/ID changes
🔤
Visible Text Matching
Locates elements by their user-visible text content — language-agnostic
🧠
LLM-Generated CSS
The AI analyzes the page snapshot and generates a new selector — covers everything else
